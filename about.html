A Resident Physician dedicated to bringing evidence-based family medicine home to Sugar Land.

IMPORTANT NOTICE: Dr. Safwan Moton is currently a Resident Physician licensed in the state of Michigan. He is not currently licensed to practice medicine in Texas. The information below reflects future intentions for his medical services in Summer 2026.

The Journey Home

Medicine has always been about community for me. My journey began right here in the greater Houston area, where I earned my Bachelor of Science from the University of Houston, graduating Magna Cum Laude. That early foundation as a Houston Louis Stokes Scholar (H-LSAMP) instilled in me a deep respect for scientific inquiry and research.

I pursued my medical training at Nova Southeastern University's Dr. Kiran C. Patel College of Osteopathic Medicine, followed by rigorous residency training with Authority Health in Michigan. While my training has taken me across the country, my goal has always been to return to Fort Bend County.

My intention is to join an established outpatient clinic in the Sugar Land or Rosenberg area where I can contribute to the community that raised me. While my specific practice location is not yet finalized, my commitment to serving the families of this region is certain.

Philosophy of Care

Empowerment Through Education

Before attending medical school, I spent years working in education, ranging from private tutoring to consulting for public school systems. This background fundamentally shapes how I practice medicine today. I believe a doctor's most important role is that of a teacher. Diagnoses can be confusing and treatment plans can feel overwhelming. I take the time to ensure my patients understand not just what medicine they are taking, but why. When you understand the "why," you are empowered to take control of your own health.

Shared Decision Making

I believe the best health outcomes happen when the physician and patient work as a team. Instead of simply dictating orders, I strive to present options and explain the risks and benefits of each path based on the latest evidence. Whether we are managing a chronic condition like hypertension or addressing an acute illness, my goal is to help you make the decision that fits your life and your values.

Evidence-Based Medicine

In an era of medical misinformation, I strive to be a trusted filter for my patients. I combine the latest clinical guidelines from major bodies (like the CDC and AAFP) with a practical approach to care. My focus is on providing high-quality, comprehensive primary care that prioritizes safety and long-term well-being.
