About Dr. Moton

Dr. Safwan Moton is a proud native of Sugar Land, Texas (Kempner High School, Class of 2006). After earning his Bachelor of Science in Biology from the University of Houston in 2011, he pursued his medical education with a commitment to primary care.

He is currently a Resident Physician at Authority Health GME in Detroit, Michigan, gaining extensive experience in urban primary care, chronic disease management, and preventive medicine.

He looks forward to returning to Fort Bend County in Summer 2026 to serve the community that raised him.
