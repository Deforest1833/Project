Meet Dr. Moton

Licensure & Certification Status: Dr. Safwan Moton is a residency-trained Family Physician currently licensed to practice medicine in the State of Michigan. He is actively in the process of relocating to Texas and acquiring his Texas medical license, with plans to open his practice in Sugar Land in Summer 2026. He is classified as Board-Eligible and is scheduled to sit for the American Board of Family Medicine certification exam in April 2026.

Dr. Moton believes that excellent primary care is built on a foundation of trust, open communication, and a commitment to evidence-based practices. He is moving to Sugar Land to establish a practice where patients are treated as partners in their health journey, not just a collection of symptoms.

While not yet seeing patients in Texas, Dr. Moton is dedicated to becoming a valuable resource for the Fort Bend County community and looks forward to welcoming families to his practice soon.

Future Areas of Care

Our practice is being built to provide comprehensive primary care for adolescents and adults. Upon opening in Summer 2026, our focus areas will include:

Preventative Medicine

Annual physicals, health screenings, and personalized wellness plans to keep you healthy before illness strikes.

Chronic Disease Management

Evidence-based management of hypertension, diabetes, high cholesterol, and other long-term conditions.

Acute Care

Evaluation and treatment for sudden illnesses, infections, and minor injuries.

*Please note: Medical services are not currently being provided. This list represents future service offerings contingent upon Texas licensure and practice opening.*
