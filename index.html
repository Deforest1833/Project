Biography

Clinical Excellence & Compassion

Dr. Safwan Moton is a Family Medicine Resident Physician distinguished by a solid foundation in evidence-based medicine and a dedication to comprehensive patient care. He is currently completing his advanced training in Michigan and plans to begin practicing in Sugar Land, Texas in July/August 2026.

Dr. Moton combines rigorous medical science with real-world clinical experience. He provides care rooted in established guidelines and tailored to effective outcomes. This commitment to clinical excellence earned him recognition as a "Top 10% Most Cited Clinician" by Doximity.
