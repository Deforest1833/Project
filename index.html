Clinical Excellence & Compassion

Dr. Safwan Moton is a Board-Eligible Family Medicine Resident Physician distinguished by a solid foundation in evidence-based medicine and a dedication to comprehensive patient care. He is currently completing his advanced training in Michigan, with plans to establish his long-term practice in Sugar Land, Texas in August 2026.

He was recognized as a "Top 10% Most Cited Clinician" by Doximity in 2024, reflecting his dedication to treatments backed by established clinical guidelines.
