About Me

I am a Board-Eligible Family Medicine physician with a passion for comprehensive, whole-person care. After completing my residency training in Michigan, I am fulfilling my long-term goal of returning home to Sugar Land, Texas to serve the community that raised me.

Family Medicine & Osteopathic Physician

Providing compassionate, evidence-based care for the entire family.

  • Degree: Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine (DO)
  • Specialty: Family Medicine
  • City: Sugar Land, TX (Starting 2026)
  • Residency: Authority Health GME
  • Medical School: Michigan State University COM
  • Email: drsafwanmoton@gmail.com

My approach to medicine is rooted in the Osteopathic philosophy that the body is a unit of body, mind, and spirit. I believe in partnering with my patients to achieve their health goals through preventative care, lifestyle management, and advanced medical treatment.
