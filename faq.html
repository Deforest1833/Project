Common Questions

Information regarding future practice plans for 2026.

IMPORTANT NOTICE: Dr. Safwan Moton is currently a Resident Physician licensed in the state of Michigan. He is not currently licensed to practice medicine in Texas and is not soliciting patients for current medical care. The information below reflects future intentions for his practice upon completion of residency and Texas licensure in Summer 2026.
Are you currently accepting new patients in Sugar Land or Rosenberg?
No. I am currently completing my residency training in Michigan. I intend to begin practicing in the Fort Bend County area (Sugar Land, Rosenberg, Richmond) in the Summer of 2026. I am not currently practicing medicine in the state of Texas.
What services will your future practice offer?
My future practice is being designed to provide comprehensive primary care for the whole family (children and adults). This will include chronic disease management (Diabetes, Hypertension), preventive screenings, and acute care for minor illnesses.
Will you provide Telemedicine or Virtual Visits?
Upon establishing my Texas practice, I plan to offer secure telemedicine options for appropriate follow-up visits and lab reviews to provide convenience for patients in the Greater Houston area.
Will you perform Sports Physicals?
Yes. I intend to offer UIL-compliant Pre-Participation Physical Evaluations for student athletes in Lamar CISD and Fort Bend ISD once my practice is open in 2026.
What is a "Medical Home"?
A "Medical Home" is a model of care where your primary physician leads a team that cares for you. It means having a doctor who knows your history, coordinates your specialist care, and helps you navigate the healthcare system, rather than just treating you when you are sick.
Do you provide Obstetrics (OB) care?
No. While I will provide general women's health and gynecological care, I do not intend to practice obstetrics or deliver babies.
What insurance plans will you accept?
Insurance credentialing will occur closer to my start date in 2026. Once I have established a practice location, I will update this website with a full list of accepted insurance providers.
